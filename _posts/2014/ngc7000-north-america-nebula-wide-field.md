This wide-field image of the North America Nebula region shows the North America Nebula, the Pelican Nebula, several other smaller nebula, and a huge number of stars.Â  Located in Cygnus, the nebula is an emission nebula located near Deneb, the tail of the Swan.

This image was consists of 30 300s exposures with a Canon T4i and 200mm EF-L lens at f/4.
